Rooted in Industry Café & Jazz
Our Story

A creative home built from culture, music, and community.

Before Industry House, there was Industry Café & Jazz — a Culver City gathering place known for eritrean cuisine, live music, creative energy, and community.

Industry House continues that legacy by opening the space to photographers, filmmakers, brands, artists, entrepreneurs, and private event hosts looking for a space with warmth, character, and purpose.

More than a venue. Industry House is a place to create meaningful work, gather with intention, and bring stories to life.

Owner & Management

The people behind the space.

Industry House is guided by the history of Industry Café & Jazz and managed with a focus on professionalism, hospitality, and creative collaboration.

Aron Tadesse

Owner, Industry Café & Jazz

Aron brings the heart of Industry Café & Jazz to Industry House, carrying forward a legacy of Eritrean cuisine, live music, culture, and community in Culver City. As a jazz musician with more than 20 years in the catering business, Aron saw a need for a community gathering spot where creatives, artists, families, and organizations could bring their dreams to life

Zerin Scales

Venue Management
